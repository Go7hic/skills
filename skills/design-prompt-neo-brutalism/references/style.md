# Neo-brutalism

- Source page: https://www.designprompts.dev/neo-brutalism
- Public route: /neo-brutalism
- Mode: light
- Accent: #FACC15
- Typography lead: sans-serif
- Style summary: A raw, high-contrast aesthetic that mimics print design and DIY punk culture. Characterized by cream backgrounds, thick black borders (4px), hard offset shadows with zero blur, clashing vibrant colors (Hot Red, Vivid Yellow, Soft Violet), and Space Grotesk typography at heavy weights. Embraces asymmetry, rotation, sticker-like layering, and organized visual chaos.
